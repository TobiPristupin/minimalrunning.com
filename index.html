Minimal Running

Minimal Running is an open source Android app that lets you track your running, and visualize your progress and evolution over time. Reach your fitness goals and boost your training, and let us do the hard work of being your fitness tracker.

Features

We stripped this fitness tracker to its essentials, so you can focus on improving yourself with the tools you need. You won’t run into pestering useless features and ad campaigns; because we don’t have any! We work for our users, not the other way round.

Store your data in the cloud

Create your account via email or using google and store your runs in the cloud, allowing you to access them in any device

Volume

Analyze your overall mileage over periods of time with animated graphs

Distribution

View the times of each one of your runs plotted on a scatterplot to analyze progress over time

Distribution

Analyze the frequency and distribution of your running over different periods of time to stay consistent with your training

Miles and Kilometers

Switch all your data between miles and kilometers with a simple click

Open Source

Minimal Running is completely open source. Downloading open source apps helps support the open source software movement dedicated to free and collaborative software.
